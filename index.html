Here's the thing about people systems

They're invisible until they break. You hire someone brilliant in interviews who struggles three months in. Your culture fragments as you scale past 100 people. Performance reviews become theater. Top performers leave without warning.

These aren't people problems. They're systems problems.

Yalin Consulting brings Industrial-Organizational Psychology to tech companies globally. We fix the invisible systems: hiring that predicts performance, leadership development that sticks, cultures built on behavior rather than posters.

Nearly a decade building these systems for gaming studios, SaaS teams, and Web3 companies across three continents. We know what breaks when you scale, and how to fix it before it does.

We build systems you own and run independently. The goal is capability, not dependency.
