1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Analyze website traffic

5. Behavioral Data We Collect

Through our analytics tools, we collect the following interaction data to improve our website:

  • Scroll depth on pages (how far you scroll)
  • Button and link clicks (which CTAs and navigation elements you interact with)
  • Time spent on pages
  • FAQ sections opened
  • Form interaction events (when you start and submit a form)
  • Page performance metrics (loading speed, layout stability)
  • Session recordings and heatmaps (via Microsoft Clarity)
  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)

This data is collected anonymously and is used solely to improve your experience on our website.
