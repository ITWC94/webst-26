Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Yalin Consulting

How quickly will I hear back?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For urgent matters or to schedule a quick discovery call, please use our contact form or book directly through Calendly.

What happens in a discovery call?

The 30-minute discovery call is a no-pressure conversation where we discuss your organizational challenges, goals, and how our Industrial-Organizational Psychology expertise can help. We'll explore whether we're a good fit and answer any questions you have about our approach.

Is the discovery call free?

Yes! The initial 30-minute discovery call is completely free with no obligations. We want to understand your needs first and ensure our services align with what you're looking for before any commitments are made.

Do you work internationally?

Absolutely. We work with companies globally, with particular expertise in Gaming, SaaS, and Web3 companies. All consultations and services can be delivered remotely. We're experienced in working across different time zones and cultural contexts.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in Gaming, SaaS, and Web3 companies, particularly startups and scaleups (50-500 employees). Our founder has deep experience in the gaming industry and understands the unique people challenges these fast-moving sectors face.

What size companies do you typically work with?

We typically work with organizations of 50-500 employees, especially those in growth phase. This is the sweet spot where scientific HR practices make the biggest impact. You're past the startup chaos but not yet at enterprise scale where you need full HR infrastructure.
