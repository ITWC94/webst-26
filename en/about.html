About Us

Our journey, our approach, and the science behind our work

Our Story

After years of placing professionals across three continents and helping companies scale, I kept seeing the same pattern: companies that hired brilliantly at 20 people watched everything break at 100. The problem was never bad people—it was systems built on gut feelings and HR practices borrowed from industries that looked nothing like theirs.

That's why I built Yalin Consulting. We bring Industrial-Organizational Psychology to gaming studios, SaaS companies, and Web3 teams—industries where context matters and generic advice falls flat.

Based in Istanbul, working globally across Europe and Asia-Pacific. We focus on three things: hiring systems that actually predict performance, learning programs that change behavior, and organizational strategies built on evidence. No templates. No borrowed "best practices." Just systems designed for how your company actually works.

Stage 01: Immersion

Week 1-2

We dive deep into your world - understanding your culture, vision, and the challenges keeping you up at night. This isn't just a discovery call. We become part of your journey, learning how your organization actually works so we can design solutions that fit your reality.

What This Looks Like:
  • In-depth conversations with leadership and team members
  • Observing how work actually gets done, not just how it's supposed to
  • Identifying the invisible systems already running
  • Understanding your constraints, culture, and context
Deliverable: Diagnostic Report & Partnership Roadmap

Stage 02: Design

Week 3-6

Based on what we learned in immersion, we design evidence-based solutions built for your specific situation. Whether it's hiring systems, learning programs, or organizational strategy, everything is created for your context. No templates. No cookie-cutter approaches.

What We Create:
  • Custom frameworks designed for your industry and stage
  • Selection systems that predict performance in your environment
  • Learning programs grounded in behavioral science
  • Organizational strategies built on evidence, not intuition
Deliverable: Complete Solution Design & Implementation Plan

Stage 03: Enable

Week 7-12

We don't just hand you a plan and disappear. We work alongside your team to implement what we've designed, training your people so they can own and run these systems independently. The goal is to build your capability, not create dependency.

How We Enable:
  • Hands-on implementation support with your team
  • Training sessions that build real competency
  • Documentation and playbooks for ongoing use
  • Knowledge transfer so you own the systems
Deliverable: Training Materials & System Documentation

Stage 04: Support

Ongoing

Once you're running the systems independently, we stay available for optimization and troubleshooting. As your organization evolves and new challenges emerge, we're here to adapt what we built together and support your continued growth.

Ongoing Support Includes:
  • Advisory support as challenges arise
  • System optimization based on results
  • Guidance on adapting to organizational changes
  • Access to expertise when you need it
Deliverable: Continuous Advisory & Optimization
